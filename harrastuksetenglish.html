Padel

There are currently more than 80,000 padel enthusiasts in Finland. Padel is a sport with a low starting threshold and it is suitable for players of all ages and levels. Padel is a racket game that combines tennis and squash and is always played as a foursome, which is why the playing teams and sociability is an essential part of the culture of the sport.


Disc golf

disc golf is perfect for a person who likes to be outdoors. The basic principle in the sport is the same as in traditional golf. In disc golf, the goal is to throw the frisbee into the goal basket with as few throws as possible. disc golf is affordable and relaxed hobby that can be played alone or in a group. You can start the sport with just a few disc golf discs, and most of the tracks are free to play


Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art, known for high and spectacular kicks, and the sport has unlimited development opportunities. Taekwondo includes a lot of hand and foot techniques and a matches. Taekwondo techniques can be applied to self-defense. In addition, it develops concentration and self-control. Taekwondo beginners do not need to be in good shape and starting it does not require any prior skills. Taekwondo is suitable for both adults and children.


Swimming

If you are looking for a hobby that you can continue throughout your life, swimming may be a suitable option. Swimming is one of the most popular sports in Finland and has many health benefits, for example it develops aerobic fitness, muscle endurance and maintains muscles. wimming is also an excellent sport for a person with a sports injury because it does not cause the strain of impact.


Crossfit

Crossfit is a form of fitness which exercises include varied and functional movements. workouts combine, for example, weight lifting, gymnastics and kettlebell movements, and running. The exercises are intense and short. The basic idea of ​​Crossfit is human functional performance development in order to cope with everyday challenges more easily. Crossfit develops e.g. strength, oxygen uptake, mobility and speed. The exercises are customizable for people of all fitness levels and ages.
